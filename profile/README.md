# MagicPods – Intelligent Wireless Audio Management for Smart Earbuds

A modern, open-source **MagicPods** solution designed to simplify wireless earbud management across devices.  
This **MagicPods** software provides real-time battery monitoring, automatic ear detection, customizable touch gestures, and seamless switching between connected devices, allowing you to focus on your audio experience without technical distractions.

## About MagicPods  
**MagicPods** is a lightweight, user‑friendly utility built specifically for true wireless stereo earbuds from leading brands.  
**MagicPods** offers deep integration with system audio services, enabling low‑latency mode, adaptive equalizer presets, and voice assistant trigger customization.  
With **MagicPods**, you get instant pop‑up notifications when opening the charging case, precise battery levels for each earbud and the case, and smart playback control based on earbud placement.  
**MagicPods** also supports firmware update notifications, connection history logs, and per‑app audio routing preferences.  
Whether you use **MagicPods** for meetings, gaming, or music, **MagicPods** adapts to your environment and improves reliability of wireless audio.  
**MagicPods** works offline, respects your privacy, and stores all settings locally.  
The **MagicPods** engine runs with minimal system resources while providing real‑time feedback through system tray integration.  
**MagicPods** is trusted by thousands of users who demand consistent performance from their true wireless earbuds.

## Key Features of MagicPods  
- **Real‑time battery tracking** – Monitor left, right, and case battery percentage through **MagicPods**.  
- **Automatic ear detection** – **MagicPods** pauses playback when earbuds are removed.  
- **Customizable touch gestures** – Assign actions (next track, voice assistant, volume) inside **MagicPods**.  
- **Low latency mode** – **MagicPods** reduces audio delay for gaming and video.  
- **Pop‑up notifications** – **MagicPods** shows connection status and battery as soon as you open the case.  
- **Per‑app audio profiles** – **MagicPods** remembers sound settings for different applications.  
- **Firmware update alerts** – **MagicPods** notifies when new earbud firmware is available.  
- **Multi‑device connection history** – **MagicPods** keeps a log of paired devices.  
- **Adaptive equalizer** – **MagicPods** adjusts sound profile based on ambient noise.  
- **Voice assistant trigger** – Launch assistant with a customizable gesture in **MagicPods**.
